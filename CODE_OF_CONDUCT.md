# Code of Conduct

This project expects contributors and participants to keep discussions respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in discussion and review.
- Assume good intent while still being clear and direct.
- Focus critique on ideas, code, and process — not people.
- Help keep the project welcoming to contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or repeated hostility
- Deliberate intimidation or bad-faith disruption
- Publishing private information without permission

## Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer through the repository’s issue tracker or GitHub contact channels. Please include enough context for the situation to be understood and reviewed.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, remove, or reject contributions and discussions that violate this policy.
